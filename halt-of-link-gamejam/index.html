Halt of Link (gamejam)

I ended up teaming with one of my colleagues, Simon Reboul, in my school for the "female Link gamejam", following Breath of the Wild reveal and internet's speculation that we could be playing a female version of Link. It was our first game jam and first project with Unity which you can play here: https://gamejolt.com/games/walk-in-the-forest/64107

I made the animations, gameplay and camera scripting and Simon made the mapping, level-design and UI. I then basically used the project as a continuous side-project where I could experience things on Unity: camera scripting, generating meshes, AI, lighting, texturing, shaders and so on…

I made some “DevLogs” on YouTube regarding the development of it, codename “Halt of Link”. The last one is dated January 2016, I did not add anything since, you can watch it on my YouTube channel: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NPb01cfSAk0
