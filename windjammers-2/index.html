Windjammers 2 (Dotemu)

Windjammers is a sports arcade game where you shoot a disc into your opponent’s goal to win. It’s fast-paced and highly addictive if you like competition: we used to compare it to Street Fighter x Pong.

I helped the team iterate through the ideas they had to enhance the original Windjammers 1994 experience. From going through new gameplay concepts to designing the AI and bringing to life the sound of the game with oversea artists, this was an intense and well rounded first experience in the industry.

Being somewhat a competitive player myself I loved the back and forth tuning we had in balancing moves and introducing mechanics, the dialog we had with the windjammers community and the passion involved. It's hard to keep a good balance when some players are rather conservative and others thriving for new stuff but we know we made good calls when they share warm emotions after a close game!
