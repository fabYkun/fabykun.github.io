My ordinary life (gamejam)

My ordinary life is our outry for the ludum dare #49. I teamed with @snowpriestess again and @yuko who is an old friend I met in RPG Maker forums... as I was still in elementary school !

The theme for this jam was "Unstable", we quickly agreed to work on a 3D environment and wanted to build a lovely world. The Unstable part would come from the characters that the player meets and the controls.

Tina worked on all the 3D static meshes, menu textures, camera settings and vehicules, Yuko worked on the characters and quest scripts and I implemented the gameplay, animations and tech. We used clayxels which is a voxel implementation simulating clay. We achieved procedural animation and were able to quickly iterate on character models thanks to this asset and we thought that it solidified the art direction. Using it for the main character was tricky at first but I think I did a great job overall with the controls and animation. We're very happy with the result despite not making as much content as we would have wanted.

Please go check our Ludum Dare webpage: https://ldjam.com/events/ludum-dare/49/my-ordinary-life
